### **Co-first Author (Listed First) - Research Project** **December 2024 – October 2025 | Published in IJBM (CAS Q2 TOP)** Led a comprehensive review of computational methods for DNA N4-methylcytosine (4mC) site prediction. Systematically analyzed the evolution of sequence modeling architectures, established standardized benchmarking pipelines, and explored multimodal fusion mechanisms and model interpretability.

#### Key Contributions: 
- **Architecture Review:** Systematically categorized machine learning and deep learning models for large-scale sequence recognition over the past five years, highlighting the evolution from traditional CNNs to pre-trained Large Language Models (LLMs). 
- **Large-Scale Benchmarking:** Built standardized sequence analysis benchmarking workflows and evaluated representative architectures like MultiScale-CNN across 6 cross-domain large-scale datasets, exploring the impact of parameter scale on model generalization. 
- **Interpretability & Representation:** Utilized tools such as kpLogo and STREME for deep analysis, revealing the correlation between specific sequence motifs (e.g., AAAA regions) and model predictive decisions, and analyzed the potential of pre-trained features in cross-species transfer learning. 
- **Multimodal Fusion & Open Source:** Explored the fusion mechanisms of multi-source heterogeneous data (sequence features, genomic positions, physicochemical properties) and their synergistic effects; open-sourced standardized inference frameworks and dataset repositories on GitHub.

### **科研项目 共同第一作者（排位第一）** **2024年12月 – 2025年10月 | IJBM 期刊已发表 (中科院二区TOP)** 负责撰写关于DNA N4-甲基胞嘧啶(4mC)位点预测计算方法的全面综述。系统梳理了序列建模架构的演进，构建了标准化基准测试，并深入探讨了模型可解释性与多模态融合机制。

#### 主要贡献: 
- **序列建模架构综述:** 系统梳理并分类了近五年内用于大规模序列识别的机器学习与深度学习模型，重点分析了从传统卷积网络(CNN)到预训练大语言模型(pre-trained LLM)的演进历程。 
- **大规模基准测试(Benchmarking):** 构建了标准化的序列分析基准测试流程，在6个跨领域的大规模数据集上对 MultiScale-CNN 等代表性前沿架构进行了性能评测，深入探讨了不同参数规模对模型泛化能力的影响。 
- **模型可解释性与表征分析:** 利用kpLogo和STREME 等工具进行深度分析，揭示了特定序列基序(如AAAA区域)的保守性与模型预测决策之间的关联，并分析了预训练特征在跨物种迁移学习中的潜力。 
- **多模态融合与领域展望:** 重点探讨了多源异构数据(如序列特征、基因组位置、物理化学性质)的融合机制及其在复杂预测任务中的协同效应；在GitHub 开源了标准化的推理框架与数据集仓库，为后续多模态表征学习的研究提供支持。
